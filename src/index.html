Kanban Board

Kanban board projects facilitate task management and workflow visualization. They consist of a board with cards representing different stages of work. Teams use cards or sticky notes to represent individual tasks or work items. Cards move across columns as tasks progress through the workflow. The board provides a real-time visual representation of the project status. The visual nature of the board helps in identifying and resolving dependencies. Kanban boards can be physical or digital, with many online tools available. Overall, Kanban boards enhance productivity and promote a continuous flow of work.
